Some types of Coffee Drinks

Cappuccino
It is usually associated with indulgence and comfort because of its thick foam layer and additional flavorings that can be added to it.
Espresso
Also known as a short black, it is approximately 1 oz. of highly concentrated coffee. Although simple in appearance, it can be difficult to master.
Iced Coffee
Iced coffee is a coffee beverage served cold. It may be prepared either by brewing coffee normally and then serving it over ice or in cold milk
Mocha
Lorem a coffee and hot chocolate hybrid. The chocolate powder or syrup gives it a rich and creamy flavor and cuts the acidity of the espresso.
